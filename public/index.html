Insights

After implementing my visualizations for Project 1, One thing I learned that I didn't learn from previous assignments was the importance of having to draw conclusions about the data that you are observing.
If I don't make sense of the dataset that I have studied, I cannot convey information about it. On top of creating visualizations to see the data, you must also apply those visualizations to real situations.
This critical skill is what makes this assignment worthwhile, and I feel that I have much to learn from here.
